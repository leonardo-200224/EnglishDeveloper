Vocabulary For Developers

Good developers know when to use technical terms and when to simplify. Shared technical vocabulary makes team discussions more efficient, but only if you also know when to translate that vocabulary for different audiences. The goal is to be understood, not to impress people with jargon. This guide covers 31 essential terms across eight categories (from programming fundamentals to cloud infrastructure), explains why shared technical vocabulary makes teams more efficient, and shows when to use precise terms versus when to translate for different audiences.
